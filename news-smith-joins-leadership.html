Press Release February 24, 2025

Elea Institute Brings Seasoned Health Care Provider on Leadership Team

Elea Institute, a national leader in advancing hospice and palliative care, has expanded its Board of Directors with the addition of Dr. Kristofer L. Smith, MD, MPP. The announcement was made on February 13, 2025.

Dr. Smith brings more than 15 years of experience in population health and value-based care to his new role. Most recently, he served as Chief Medical Officer of Optum at Home. His previous leadership positions include Chief Medical Officer at Landmark Health, Chief Clinical Officer at Prospero Health, President of naviHealth's Home-based Medical Care division, and Senior Vice President of Population Health at Northwell Health. Early in his medical career, Dr. Smith worked as a house-call physician focused on primary and palliative care.

"Kristofer's experience as a physician and leader in palliative care, combined with his commitment to transforming healthcare delivery, aligns perfectly with our vision for improving hospice care nationwide."
— Kent Mathy, Chairman of the Board, Elea Institute
"End-of-life care is one of the most important places in healthcare to help patients and caregivers. I look forward to advancing hospice care through collaboration with the Elea Institute team."
— Dr. Kristofer L. Smith, MD, MPP

About Elea Institute

Elea Institute is a nonprofit organization dedicated to expanding access to high-quality hospice and palliative care through grant funding, research development, clinical resources, and educational initiatives.
