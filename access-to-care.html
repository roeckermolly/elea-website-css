Improving Access to Quality Care

Our Aspirations

Elea Institute strives to ensure that all patients have access to comprehensive, high-quality hospice and palliative care. We provide philanthropic support to organizational partners to develop resources that empower patients and caregivers to help them successfully navigate the healthcare system in order to access care and better manage the complexities of end-of-life care.

Elea's grant funding and partnerships support initiatives, including:

  • Identifying and working to reduce barriers to coverage for care
  • Identifying quality in-home care opportunities
  • Supporting advanced care planning
  • Providing training and resources to caregivers
