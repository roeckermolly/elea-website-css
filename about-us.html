About Us

Dedicated leadership committed to advancing hospice and palliative care across the nation.

Our Story

Our History

The seedlings of Elea Institute began in 1978 when Horizon Hospice and Midwest Palliative & Hospice CareCenter were founded in Chicago. These entities merged to become JourneyCare.

In 2022, JourneyCare was acquired by Addus HomeCare, enabling the creation of Elea Institute to build on the collective histories and legacies of dedicated healthcare professionals and volunteers.

The organization remains committed to ensuring all persons have access to high quality hospice and palliative care, and has awarded numerous grants supporting enhanced community access and educational resources.
