Elea Institute: Pioneering National Philanthropic Leadership in Expanding Access to Hospice and Palliative Care

Elea Institute has emerged as a national leader in advancing access to high-quality hospice and palliative care. Created following a merger of three clinical hospice and palliative care providers, the organization's work is grounded in the foundational values of dignity, compassion, and comfort.

The institute provides comprehensive resources—including industry research, educational materials, grant opportunities, and case studies—for patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals nationwide.

"Elea Institute is a cornerstone in the hospice and palliative care community, dedicated to expanding care access nationwide through strategic partnerships."
— Kent Mathy, Chair of the Board
"People are at the center of everything we do. Through research, education, advocacy, and collaboration, we work to make certain that every patient and caregiver has the support they need."
— Kelly Kenney, Board Member

Core Philanthropic Objectives

Elea Institute's grantmaking and initiatives are organized around three strategic pillars:

  • Improving Access to Quality Care
  • Advancing Research, Education, and Training
  • Increasing Awareness of End-of-Life Care

These objectives reflect Elea Institute's commitment to addressing the field's most pressing challenges—from workforce development and care coordination to policy reform and public awareness—through strategic investment and partnership.

About Elea Institute

Elea Institute is a nonprofit organization investing in critical research, clinical resources development, and educational opportunities. The organization creates positive impacts for patients, caregivers, providers, and the broader healthcare system through grant funding and strategic initiatives.
