About Me

A Sales-First Mindset

I'm a sales professional with a proven track record of building pipelines, earning trust, and closing revenue. Based in Washington, D.C., I currently work at Aircall, where I help businesses unlock the full potential of their sales communications.

My approach to sales is rooted in understanding the customer — their challenges, their goals, and the specific moment they're ready to buy. I've built and managed relationships across multiple markets, and I believe consistent process and genuine curiosity are what separate good reps from great ones.

Outside of work, I'm committed to community involvement. At UC San Diego I was a founding member of MASAR, an organization dedicated to educating men about sexual misconduct and consent — a cause I care about deeply.
